Frequently Asked Questions

How does the one-time monthly payment work? +
FLUXS operates on a one-time monthly billing model. You pay once for 30 days of access. No recurring charges. No auto-renewal. When your 30 days expire, you can choose to renew for another month or cancel. Complete control, zero lock-in.
Can I cancel or pause my subscription? +
Yes. Since there's no subscription, you simply don't renew when your 30-day period ends. No cancellation forms. No phone calls. Access terminates naturally when your paid period expires.
What happens after my 30-day access expires? +
Your access ends. All historical data and insights remain accessible if you renew. Simply purchase another month when you're ready to continue using FLUXS.
Is there a free trial? +
One free flux assessment is available without payment. After that, access requires a paid subscription. This demonstrates the quality and accuracy of our algorithm before you commit.
What is the refund policy? +
No refunds after your one-time monthly payment is processed. This commitment ensures you invest fully in tracking and optimizing your peak state. Each month is a fresh choice to continue.
Do I need to provide a credit card for the free assessment? +
No. The free assessment requires only your inputs. Payment is only needed if you choose to purchase a subscription tier after experiencing the assessment.
Can I share access with my team? +
Yes. Elite Team (5 members) and iLead Premium (unlimited members) include multi-user access. Each tier is designed for collaboration. Solo (Pro) is for individual use only.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. Payments are secure, encrypted, and PCI-compliant.
